## The Serious Part (Yes, It Exists)

On the technical front, I’m genuinely obsessed with distributed systems, databases, and architectures that don’t collapse the moment real traffic shows up — the kind of foundational work (GFS, Spanner, the classics) that still holds up decades later.  

Right now I’m building my own distributed database from scratch.  
The goal is stupidly high: scale that doesn’t flinch, reliability that laughs at failure, and availability that actually means something.  
It’s ambitious, borderline delusional, and honestly one of the coolest things I’ve committed to. When I decide something should last, it usually does.
